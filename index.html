Frequently Asked Questions

When do the 5X rates activate?
Friday 6PM EST through Sunday 11PM EST. Weekdays are 2X rates so casuals can still farm.
Are blueprints wiped?
Yes, full BP wipe every Thursday 2PM EST. Everyone starts fresh. No sweaty groups with everything researched day 1.
Can I play solo?
Absolutely. We don't have solo-only rules but the 8-man limit keeps mega zergs out. Solos and duos thrive here.
Do you ban offline raiders?
No. Offline raiding is part of Rust. But we do have raid windows during peak hours where you get bonus explosives.
How do I report cheaters?
Open a ticket in Discord. Include video evidence if possible. Admins review reports within 24 hours. We have zero tolerance.
Is there a grace period after wipe?
First 2 hours after Thursday wipe = no raiding. After that, it's open season. Get your base up quick.
Can I join mid-wipe?
Yes! Weekend 5X rates make it easy to catch up. Plus we have starter kits in the Discord for new joiners.
What map size?
4000 map with 200 slots. Big enough to spread out, small enough for constant action. Perfect balance.
